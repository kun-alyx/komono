おしらせ (Pixiv Fanbox)

Published:

Imported:

Content

This is not about the new Prohibited Items on Pixiv.

PayPal has announced that the sending and receiving of payments between Pixiv and PayPal accounts in my country will be suspended as of December 15th, 2022.

Pixiv stated that they are working on launching a new payment system, so I waited, but there is currently no other way to receive payment from Fanbox.


At this rate, I can no longer open the Fanbox, so, I decided to open buymeacoffee.

This is a hard dicision but I'll delete all my Fanbox plans soon. I'll keep this Fanbox if I can in the case that Pixiv has a new payment system in the future.


I got a lot of support here that made my days for almost a year that I've opened this Fanbox and I can't say enough thanks for all your support.


Please consider supporting or joining membership on my buymeacoffee if you are able.


Thank you so much for all your support again and again.
