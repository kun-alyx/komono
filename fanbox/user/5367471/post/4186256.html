I'd like to apologize to all of my supporters for the lack of updates this month. I'm currently working on a short doujin about boys in PE uniforms. So I'd like to show how far it's come.


I've never felt confident drawing b/w manga, but by drawing this, I feel like I've learned a lot of things little by little.


I hope you enjoy it.



PS: The names of the bois are AO (blue hair) and Nori (Green hair). I hadn't given them names before, but I liked them and drew them a lot during Kinktober last year. (You can find all of my Kinktober posts on Twitter and Baraag.)
